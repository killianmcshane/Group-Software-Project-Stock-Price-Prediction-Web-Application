What does this mean?

This graph shows the fluctuations of the particular stock you have selected. We use the previous behaviour of the stock in our calculations when trying to predict wether or not the stock will go up or down. This prediction is then also shown in the graph above. NOTE: these are just our predictions and therefore G4SP do not take any responsibility for any users buying into a stock that then does not do what we have predicted. I.E makes a loss when we predicted it to make a profit.
